1. 可以指定回放值的数量

        var subject = new Rx.AsyncSubject();
        subject.subscribe({{'{'}}
                next: (v) => console.log('observerA: ' + v)
        {{'}'}});
        subject.next(1);
        subject.next(2);
        subject.next(3);
        subject.next(4);

        subject.subscribe({{'{'}}
                next: (v) => console.log('observerB: ' + v)
        {{'}'}});

        subject.next(5);
        subject.complete();
      
    
      输出结果如下：
        observerA: 5
        observerB: 5
